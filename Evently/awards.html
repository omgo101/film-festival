Judging Criteria

Our esteemed jury evaluates films based on the following criteria

Originality & Creativity

Innovative storytelling, unique perspective, and creative approach to the subject matter.

Technical Merit

Adaptability of techniques to strengthen the narratives.

Art of Storytelling

Engaging narratives that captivate audiences.

Emotional Impact

Resonance with viewers' feelings and experiences.
