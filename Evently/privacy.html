Privacy Policy

This tells you what data we collect and how we use it.

1) Information We Collect

  • Personal: Name, email, phone, address, age, payment details.
  • Submissions: Film title, synopsis, production details, promo materials.
  • Ticketing & Payment: Billing details for tickets/fees/sponsorships.
  • On-Site: Photos/videos captured during events.
  • Technical: IP, browser, device info when you visit our site.

2) How We Use Data

  • Process submissions, ticket sales, and payments.
  • Send festival updates, schedules, and news.
  • Manage hospitality/logistics for guests, jurors, filmmakers.
  • Issue prizes, citations, certificates.
  • Improve our website and audience experience.
  • Follow legal rules.

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share with:
