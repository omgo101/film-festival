Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Oct 08, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Hyderabad International Short Film Festival collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you interact with our festival, website, and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number, postal address, age, and payment details when you register, buy tickets, or submit films.

Submission Details

Information provided by filmmakers including film title, synopsis, production details, and promotional materials.

Ticketing & Payment Information

Billing details for tickets (₹500 passes, submission fees, or sponsorship payments).

On-Site Data

Photos and videos taken during the festival events, which may include attendees and participants.

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, device information collected when visiting our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process film submissions, ticket sales, and payments
  • Send festival updates, schedules, and announcements
  • Manage hospitality and logistics for guests, jurors, and filmmakers
  • Issue prizes, citations, and certificates to winners and participants
  • Improve our website, marketing, and audience experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Payment gateways for processing secure transactions
  • Event partners, sponsors, and service providers strictly to manage the festival
  • Government or legal authorities if required by law
  • Media outlets and our official channels for sharing event highlights, photos, and videos

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to:

  • Use secure servers and encrypted payment gateways
  • Limit access to personal information to authorized staff only
  • Protect against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse of data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Our festival does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

You can contact us at info@hyderabadfilmfest.com for any privacy-related requests including:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your information
  • Objection to processing of your data

7. Photography & Media Consent

By attending HISFF 2025 events, you agree that your image or video may be captured and used for:

  • Event promotion (posters, brochures, social media, and press releases)
  • Archival and documentary purposes related to the festival

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on our website with the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us
