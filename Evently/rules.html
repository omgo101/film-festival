Rules & Regulations

Read this before you submit your short film.

Opens

Oct 15, 2025

Deadline

Nov 20, 2025

Notification

Dec 5, 2025

Fee

₹500 (non-refundable)

Submit on FilmFreeway See Awards

  • Open to short films of all genres.
  • Max runtime: 25 minutes (including credits). No minimum length.
  • Films completed after January 1, 2023 are eligible.
  • Any language is okay, but English subtitles must be clear and readable.
  • Current/past students or faculty of Dadasaheb Phalke School of Film Studies are not eligible.

  • Submit via FilmFreeway or the official HISFF website.
  • Submission fee: ₹500 (non-refundable).
  • Registration opens: October 15, 2025.
  • Final deadline: November 20, 2025.
  • Provide a screener link (Vimeo or private Google Drive link).
  • For screening, we prefer high-quality DCP / ProRes / MP4.

  • Poster (Horizontal): PNG/JPG, min 1920×1080
  • Poster (Vertical): PNG/JPG, min 1080×1500
  • Teaser: MP4, max 10s
  • Trailer: MP4, max 90s
  • Synopsis: 100–150 words

  • Selection Committee shortlists films for Competition/Showcase.
  • Preview committee shortlists for screening; Jury decides awards.
  • All committee decisions are final.
  • Notifications by December 5, 2025 (email + FilmFreeway).

  • 1st Prize: $1,100 / ₹100,000
  • 2nd Prize: $800 / ₹75,000
  • 3rd Prize: $500 / ₹50,000
  • Promising Endeavour (I): $200 / ₹20,000
  • Promising Endeavour (II): $200 / ₹20,000
  • Jury Special Mention (I): $100 / ₹10,000
  • Jury Special Mention (II): $100 / ₹10,000
  • People’s Choice Award (1): Trophy & Certificate
Awards include Plaques and Citation Certificates of Merit. Jury’s decisions are final.

See full Awards & Prizes

  • Festival is not responsible for any copyright infringement.
  • Submitter must hold and indemnify all legal rights.
  • By submitting, you grant HISFF a non-exclusive screening right during the festival.
  • Selected films may also be used for roadshows/education/promotion (with prior intimation).
  • Copyright stays with the filmmaker.

  • Accepted: DCP, ProRes, or high-quality MP4 (1080p min, 4K preferred).
  • Preferred frame rate: 24 fps.
  • Burned-in English subtitles required for all films.
  • Ensure your file is free of technical errors.

  • Submitting means you accept all festival rules.
  • We may reject films that violate legal, cultural, or ethical guidelines.
  • No films promoting hate speech, explicit pornography, or political propaganda.
