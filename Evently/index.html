Hyderabad International Short Film Festival

Where stories from Andhra Pradesh and Telangana meet the world.

The Hyderabad International Short Film Festival (HISFF), presented by the Dadasaheb Phalke School of Film Studies, is the first short film festival of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana — a landmark initiative designed to celebrate the power of short storytelling.

Edition 1 marks the beginning of a long journey to inspire, innovate, and empower filmmakers to create films of impact from the two states and beyond.
