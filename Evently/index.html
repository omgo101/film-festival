Hyderabad Short Film Festival

The Hyderabad Short Film Festival (HSFF) is conceived as an annual, marquee cultural event in Telangana by Dadasaheb Phalke School of Film Studies. This festival aims at uniting global cinema, regional storytelling, and industry linkages providing a platform for the youth.

Over 2 days each year, HSFF will host film screenings (short films), panel discussions, and heritage screenings. By embedding Telangana's cultural identity into the festival, HSFF aims to position Hyderabad as a global film hub, promote local creative talent (especially youth), spur economic and tourism benefits, and strengthen the state's ecosystem pertaining film.
