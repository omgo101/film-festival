Main Venue

Prasad’s Multiplex, Hyderabad

Necklace Road, Khairtabad, Hyderabad, Telangana 500082

Modern multiplex with professional projection and sound. Two large auditoriums are reserved for the festival over two days, with full technical crew support.

Venue Location

By Air

Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (HYD). Take airport cabs or TSRTC Pushpak to city; ~35–50 mins depending on traffic.

By Train

Hyderabad Deccan (Nampally) and Secunderabad Jn are the closest majors. Taxis/metros easily available to the venue.

By Road

App-based cabs & parking options nearby. Plan extra time around weekday peak hours.
