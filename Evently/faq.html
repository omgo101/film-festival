Hyderabad International Short Film Festival 2025

Simple, clear information to help you plan your visit and submissions.

General

HISFF 2025 is the Hyderabad International Short Film Festival. It celebrates short films and creative talent from India and around the world.

The planned venue is Prasads Multiplex, Hyderabad. See full details on the Venue page.

Filmmakers, students, industry professionals, press, and film lovers. We expect a large and diverse audience.

Film Submissions

Submit through the platform linked on our home or tickets page. Follow the steps and upload your film with required details.

Your film must be original. You must have rights for all music, images, and footage used. Full rules are on the Terms page.

Yes, a submission fee applies. Prices are available on the official website.

Tickets & Passes

Visit the Tickets page and follow the checkout process. You’ll get an email or SMS confirmation.

Ticket categories and prices may change. Please check the official website for current prices.

Tickets are generally non-refundable. See details on the Terms page.

Awards & Jury

We offer multiple awards and special mentions. See Awards & Prizes for categories. Prize amounts, if any, are shown on the website.
