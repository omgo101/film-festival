Venue & Technical Support

Location

Prasad’s Multiplex

  • Two large auditoriums / screening halls (2 days)
  • Staging, lighting infrastructure and seating

Technical Setup (Jury Screening)

  • Projectors & Screens
  • Sound system, microphones & mixing
  • Lighting setup
  • Skilled technicians & backup equipment

Hospitality Overview

  • 3‑night hotel stay for invited filmmakers & jurors with local transport
  • Meals for guests & crew with tea/coffee breaks (2 days)
  • Local pickups & drops, and reimbursements for out‑of‑town guests
