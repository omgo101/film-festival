About

Hyderabad International Short Film Festival (HISFF) is a 3‑day celebration of short films, culture and creativity in Hyderabad.

  1. Home
  2. About

About HISFF 2025

HISFF is conceived by the Dadasaheb Phalke School of Film Studies as a marquee cultural event for Telangana—uniting global cinema and regional storytelling, and giving a strong platform to youth and new voices.

Over three days, the festival features competitive and non‑competitive screenings, special pavilions, and heritage/retrospective programming, along with Q&As, panels and networking. The aim is to position Hyderabad as a global film hub while growing the local ecosystem.

"We bridge cultures, spark conversations, and nurture creativity through the art of film."

About Our Institution

Dadasaheb Phalke School of Film Studies was launched in 2017, with the desire to give quality Film education and in creating a solid platform that will raise incredible talent who will decorate the Telugu film industry.

We have 243 students studied since our launch and we strive to enhance the quality of the all-around film in the industry.
