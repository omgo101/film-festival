Festival Jury

Our distinguished panel of jury members who will evaluate the competitive short films

Jury Member

Nagesh Kukunoor

Writer & Director

Acclaimed Indian filmmaker known for his unique storytelling in films like "Hyderabad Blues", "Iqbal", and "Dor". His work often explores social issues with sensitivity and depth.

Jury Member

Naga Ashwin

Writer & Director

Visionary Telugu filmmaker recognized for his innovative approach to cinema. Known for pushing creative boundaries while maintaining strong narrative structures in his films.

Jury Member

Nasser

Actor, Director, President of Nadigar Sangam

Veteran Indian actor with a career spanning decades across multiple languages. Known for his powerful performances and contributions to cinema as both artist and industry leader.

Jury Member

Tharun Bhaskar

Writer & Director

Emerging filmmaker known for his fresh perspective and innovative storytelling techniques. His work demonstrates a unique blend of traditional narratives with contemporary themes.

Jury Member

Revathi

Actor, Writer, Director

Renowned Indian actress and filmmaker known for her powerful performances and directorial ventures. A strong advocate for women in cinema and meaningful storytelling.

Jury Member

Manju Borah

Writer & Director

Award-winning Assamese filmmaker known for her sensitive portrayal of human relationships and social issues. Her films have received critical acclaim at national and international festivals.
